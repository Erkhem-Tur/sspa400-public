ALC Book 4 Lesson 1 — Sports Event Protection + Simple Past

Mission: Use sports vocabulary and regular past tense to report a protected public sports event.

ALC focus: Sports, invitations, regular past -ed, Did questions, time expressions.

SSPA context: SSPA checkpoint language, protected area phrases, radio/SITREP reporting, public event control.

VocabularyGrammarIndexBK4 L1 BK4 L2 BK4 L3 BK5 L1

How this lesson integrates ALC + SSPA handbook

1. Learn ALC language

Vocabulary and grammar are taught from the ALC lesson.

2. Add operational context

Students apply the same grammar to protection, checkpoint, duty, service, and report situations.

3. Perform professionally

Every task ends with a role-play, duty report, or service conversation.

Grammar: Regular Past and Did Questions

Regular past -ed

Finished action: We checked IDs yesterday. They played volleyball after duty.

Болж өнгөрсөн үйлдэл: checked, played, inspected.

Did questions

Did + subject + base verb? Did you check the entrance?

Did-ийн дараа check гэж үндсэн хэлбэрээр орно.

Time expressions

Use yesterday, last night, this morning, at 0900 hours.

Хэзээ болсон гэдгийг тодорхой хэлнэ.

SITREP sentence frame

Control, this is Team One. We checked the entrance at 0900. No incident. Over.

Радиогоор богино, тод, баримттай мэдээлнэ.

Scenario cards

  1. Checkpoint at a protected basketball game
  2. After-event duty report
  3. Radio SITREP from Team One
  4. Invite a colleague to play volleyball after shift

Teacher modeling sequence

  1. Teacher says the model twice: slow → natural speed.
  2. Class repeats chorally.
  3. Students identify vocabulary + grammar.
  4. Pairs substitute rank, place, time, item, and purpose.
  5. Students perform without reading.
  6. Teacher corrects: pronunciation → grammar → professional tone.
Багш эхлээд загварлан хэлнэ, сурагчид нийтээр давтана, дараа нь өөрсдийн нөхцөлд сольж ярьж гүйцэтгэнэ.

Model dialogue

Open model

Officer: Good morning, sir/ma’am. How can I help you?

Visitor/Colleague: Good morning. I am here for the training/event at 0900.

Officer: May I check your ID, permission, or badge please?

Visitor/Colleague: Yes, here it is.

Officer: Thank you. Please follow the instructions.

Нэр, цол, цаг, зорилго, эд зүйл, чиглэлийг сольж бодит яриа болгоно.

Quick quiz

  2. Did you ____ the visitor’s bag? Answer: inspect
  3. Visitors walked through the ____. Answer: metal detector
