ALC Book 4 Lesson 2 — Ranks, Duty, Military Time + SSPA Self-Introduction

Mission: Introduce rank and job, use irregular past verbs, and report for duty using 24-hour time.

ALC focus: Military personnel, ranks, irregular past, Did questions, military time.

SSPA context: SSPA ranks/jobs, self-introduction, duty posts, checkpoint/protected area duties.

How this lesson integrates ALC + SSPA handbook

1. Learn ALC language

Vocabulary and grammar are taught from the ALC lesson.

2. Add operational context

Students apply the same grammar to protection, checkpoint, duty, service, and report situations.

3. Perform professionally

Every task ends with a role-play, duty report, or service conversation.

Grammar: Irregular Past and Did Questions

Irregular past

go→went, come→came, get→got, have→had, eat→ate, drink→drank, see→saw, do→did, give→gave.

Тогтмол бус үйл үгийг цээжилнэ.

Did + base verb

Did you come on time? Yes, I did. I came at 0650.

Асуултад come, go, have гэх мэт үндсэн хэлбэр орно.

Self-introduction

My name is ____. My rank is ____. I work at the SSPA as a ____. I am on duty today.

Нэр, цол, байгууллага, албан тушаал, үүргээ хэлнэ.

Military time

0700 = zero seven hundred; 1500 = fifteen hundred; 2130 = twenty-one thirty.

Цагийг 24 цагийн хэлбэрээр тод хэлнэ.

Scenario cards

Model dialogue

Open model

Officer: Good morning, sir/ma’am. How can I help you?

Visitor/Colleague: Good morning. I am here for the training/event at 0900.

Officer: May I check your ID, permission, or badge please?

Visitor/Colleague: Yes, here it is.

Officer: Thank you. Please follow the instructions.

Нэр, цол, цаг, зорилго, эд зүйл, чиглэлийг сольж бодит яриа болгоно.

Quick quiz

  1. Yesterday we ____ to the training ground. Answer: went
  2. Did she ____ on time? Answer: come
  3. My ____ is first lieutenant. Answer: rank
  4. I ____ lunch at 1200. Answer: ate / had
  5. 0900 is ____ hundred. Answer: zero nine
