ALC Book 4 Lesson 3 — Clothes, Uniform Rules, Can/Must/May

Mission: Describe clothing/uniforms and explain ability, requirements, prohibition, and permission in protected areas.

ALC focus: Clothes, put on/take off, can/can’t, must/must not, may/can permission, punctuation.

SSPA context: SSPA dress code, ID badge visibility, restricted area rules, prohibited photography/items.

Grammar: Can, May, Must, and Must Not

Can / can’t

Ability or possibility: I can use the radio. Visitors can go upstairs on the right side.

Чадвар эсвэл боломж.

Must

Requirement: You must keep your permission badge exposed.

Заавал хийх шаардлага.

Must not / cannot

Prohibition: You must not take pictures here. You cannot go through here.

Хориг, болохгүй зүйл.

May / Can permission

May I check your personal belongings? Can I enter?

Зөвшөөрөл эелдгээр асуух.

Punctuation in dialogue

Use capital letters, commas, periods, question marks. “May I check your ID?”

Яриа бичихэд цэг тэмдэглэлийг зөв хэрэглэнэ.

Scenario cards

Model dialogue

Open model

Officer: Good morning, sir/ma’am. How can I help you?

Visitor/Colleague: Good morning. I am here for the training/event at 0900.

Officer: May I check your ID, permission, or badge please?

Visitor/Colleague: Yes, here it is.

Officer: Thank you. Please follow the instructions.
