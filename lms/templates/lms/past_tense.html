🔵 Present Simple — Одоогийн цаг
USE: Habits, routines, facts
+ I/You/We/They + verb  |  He/She/It + verbs
− do/does + not + verb
"I check badges every day."
"The SSPA protects the President."
🔴 Simple Past — Өнгөрсөн цаг
USE: Completed actions, past events
Regular: verb + -ed
Irregular: special form (see table)
"I checked his badge yesterday."
"He went to the checkpoint."

Mr. Rashid's Vacation — Story
Mr. Rashid went on vacation last month. His flight was terrible — it was so bumpy and very scary. When he arrived, the weather was terrible. He never saw the sun. He stayed inside the hotel every day. His room was fine but the music from a nearby café was very loud — he didn't sleep much. The food was too salty and the waiters were very unfriendly. Then someone stole his wallet! After that he stayed in the hotel and read a book. To make things worse, they cancelled his flight home. He had to stay for two more days. But — there was one good thing! He met a very nice woman named Basmar. He is seeing her tonight! 😊
